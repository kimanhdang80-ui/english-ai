## Investigated and NOT a bug (documented to prevent re-investigation)

- **Daily-loop explanation id-parsing** — `^(?:mc|fb|tf)-(.+)$` (`daily-lesson-service.ts`) vs quiz
  ids `mc-/fb-/tf-${item.id}` (`quiz.ts`): all match; `(.+)` correctly captures UUIDs with internal
  hyphens. `match-${g}` deliberately doesn't match (gets the generic explanation) and is sliced off
  before surfacing (`DEFAULT_QUIZ_COUNT=5`). **Correct**, and covered by `daily-lesson-service.test.ts`.
- **SRS scheduler** (`domain/srs.ts`) — SM-2-lite verified line-by-line: `again` resets interval/reps
  and reduces ease with a lapse; ease clamped to 1.3; graduating intervals 1→3→interval×ease; status
  thresholds (7d known, 30d mastered) consistent. **No correctness bug.**
- **Response envelope / status mapping** (`src/lib/http/response.ts`) — `STATUS_BY_CODE` maps domain
  errors correctly (404/400/401/409/501/500) and never leaks internals. **Correct.**
