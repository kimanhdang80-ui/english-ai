## How to use

- New discoveries get the next `DEBT-###` id, a severity, and status `OPEN`.
- To act on an item, move it to `SCHEDULED` and add a plan entry in
  [`docs/REFACTOR_PLAN.md`](../docs/REFACTOR_PLAN.md); mark `PARTIAL`/`RESOLVED` when the
  owning sprint completes it (note "how" in that sprint's `technical-debt.md`).
